BeastValidator is available via NPM, Composer, or CDN. Choose the method that fits your stack.

üîß NPM

üêò Composer

üåê CDN

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/beastvalidator@latest/dist/beastvalidator.min.css">
üß† Basic Usage
